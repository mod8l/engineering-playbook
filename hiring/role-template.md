## Title

[Staff / Principal] Machine Learning Engineer — [Team or Area]

## Location

[Remote / Hybrid / On-site] — [Office location if applicable]

## Reporting To

[Title of hiring manager]

## The Role

We are looking for a [Staff/Principal] Machine Learning Engineer to [one-sentence mission]. You will own the design, delivery, and operational health of ML systems that [business outcome]. This is a [individual-contributor / people-adjacent] role that partners closely with [product, research, platform, data, etc.] teams.

## What You Will Do

- Architect and ship [type of ML systems, e.g., recommendation, NLP, forecasting, computer-vision] systems that are reliable, observable, and maintainable.
- Lead the technical strategy for [domain area] in close collaboration with [stakeholders].
- Drive cross-team standards for [model evaluation, feature engineering, MLOps, experimentation, responsible AI].
- Mentor engineers and raise the bar for technical design, code review, and incident response.
- Translate ambiguous product or research goals into concrete, iterative engineering plans.
- Identify and reduce technical risk before it becomes a blocker.

## Impact Expectations

| Level | Scope | Typical Impact |
|-------|-------|----------------|
| Staff | Team + adjacent teams | Drives outcomes across [2-4] teams; defines patterns others adopt. |
| Principal | Organization-wide | Shapes multi-year technical direction; influences company-level strategy. |

## Core Competencies

- **ML system design.** Can design end-to-end systems that balance model quality, latency, cost, and maintainability.
- **Software engineering.** Writes production code, designs clean APIs, and understands testing, observability, and deployment.
- **Data judgment.** Recognizes leakage, distribution shift, labeling issues, and bias risks before they reach production.
- **Communication.** Explains complex trade-offs to technical and non-technical audiences.
- **Leadership without authority.** Aligns peers and stakeholders around a technical direction.

## Leveling Notes

- A Staff candidate may be stronger in some competencies and developing in others; breadth and impact trajectory matter more than checking every box.
- A Principal candidate should show repeated evidence of organization-wide impact and the ability to make good bets under uncertainty.
- We value non-traditional paths. Research, product engineering, platform, and open-source experience can all be relevant.

## Nice-to-Have

- Experience with [specific tech stack or domain relevant to the role].
- Track record of publishing, speaking, or contributing to open-source ML projects.
- Familiarity with regulated or high-stakes domains such as [healthcare, finance, safety-critical systems].
