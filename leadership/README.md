# Leadership

This section is for R&D leaders who are asked to turn research into revenue: VP R&D, CTOs, heads of machine learning, and technical founders who need their teams to ship to production.

Engineering playbooks usually stop at process. Leadership artifacts are where process meets money, time, and people. They are the documents I reach for when a board asks why R&D needs another platform hire, when a founder asks whether to split the research team from the product team, or when an investor wants a one-page scorecard before writing a term sheet.

The opinions here are strong because the decisions are expensive. Every recommendation is a starting point; adapt it to your market, your team, and your risk tolerance.
