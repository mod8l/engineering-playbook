[![Markdown Lint](https://github.com/gadsosa/engineering-playbook/actions/workflows/markdown-lint.yml/badge.svg)](https://github.com/gadsosa/engineering-playbook/actions/workflows/markdown-lint.yml)

A public, employer-agnostic playbook for R&D leadership: how I hire, review code, respond to incidents, and adopt AI responsibly.

This repo is a living set of opinions and templates, not a rulebook. Every organization has different constraints, so treat each page as a starting point for conversation rather than a mandate. I have written it in first-person voice where it felt natural, because leadership practices are ultimately personal: they reflect what I have seen work, what I have seen fail, and what I am still learning.

## How to Use This Repo

1. **Read the principles first.** The [R&D principles](r-and-d-principles.md) are the spine; everything else flows from them.
2. **Pick a practice area.** Each section has a `README.md` that explains intent and links to concrete templates.
3. **Fork the templates.** Replace bracketed placeholders and adapt checklists to your context.
4. **Challenge the content.** If a template does not fit your team, open an issue or propose a change. Good process is discovered, not decreed.

## Contributing

I welcome corrections, clarifications, and new perspectives. Because this repo is intentionally non-proprietary, please do not submit internal company documents, proprietary data, or employer-specific anecdotes. Keep examples fictional and employer-agnostic.
