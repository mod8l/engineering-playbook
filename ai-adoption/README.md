# AI Adoption

Adopting AI is not a technology decision; it is a product, risk, and operations decision. I default to skepticism about hype and optimism about disciplined experimentation.

This section contains:

- [Evaluation Framework](evaluation-framework.md) — build vs buy vs partner decision matrix.
- [Responsible AI Checklist](responsible-ai-checklist.md) — safety, privacy, bias, transparency, and oversight.
- [Experiment Log Template](experiment-log-template.md) — how to log and learn from AI experiments.

## Guiding Principles

- **Start with the problem, not the model.** AI is a means, not an end.
- **Measure end-to-end impact.** Offline metrics are necessary but not sufficient.
- **Design for failure.** Models drift, hallucinate, and degrade. Plan for it.
- **Keep humans in the loop for high-stakes decisions.** Automation is powerful; accountability still belongs to people.
